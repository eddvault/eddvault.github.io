Wanted/Hancock/Kung Fu Panda/Harold & Kumar 2

Posted Jul 6th 2008, 9:50PM
So yeah, i have recently been seeing a number of films due to the fact college is finished and friend are comign back from university and what not, so here are my thoughts...

Harold & Kumar: Escape from Guantanamo Bay
I liked this pretty much the same as the first Harold & Kumar film, it was a funny film to watch but its hardly the most well written comedy. But there was enough to keep me entertained for te films duration
3/5

Wanted
Before i saw this film, i really didnt want to go and see it. The thought of yet another film where Angelina Jolie acts all badass and mysterious didnt sound all to tempting.
But as soon as the film showed that first stunt with those special effects, i soon changed my mind. It had some great direction with the special effects. Most of it was pretty predictable, but the effects were very impressive. Kid of like the first time you watch 'The Matrix'.
4/5

Hancock
Starts off just as you expect, and seeing Will Smith act like a bastard superhero was fun. Then as the plot develops, by the end it was less of a comedy. It wasn't neccesairaly a bad thing, it just changed the tone of the film dramatically in my opinion.
3.5/5

Kung Fu Panda
Unlike usual Dreamworks films (Shrek 2, Shrek 3, Over the hedge), this one was actually quite impressive story and animation wise.. I especially loved the 2D Animated segments they had at the start and the end of the movie. Look out for those if you go see it!
4/5

So yeah thats my opinion on those films, not that anyone wants it!
