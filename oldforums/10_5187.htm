Golden Rule: Don't be Evil!

Remember, if you have any problem, be sure to notify a moderator.

Breaking the following banned content will result in a permanent ban, usually without warning:

    1-Violence or racial intolerance.
    2-Illicit drugs or drug paraphernalia.
    3-Pornographic, XXX material, exploitation of minors, adult, babes or mature content.
    4-Excessive profanity/vulgar languages.
    The degree of the excessiveness will be determined by the Moderator, if you don't want to be banned for this offense, keep your swearing down to Curse Words.
    5-Hacking/cracking content.
    6- Illegal MP3/Movie/Applications/Torrent/P2P/Emule link (It is also illegal to provide links to illegal files hosted on other websites).
    7-Illicit or illegal content (Including yahoo rares, yahoo cracks, etc).
    8-Bigotry, racism, hatred, material which may be insulting to another person(s) or company.
    9- Copyrighted material, trademarks, trade secrets or other intellectual property rights used without proper authorization, material that is obscene, defamatory, constitutes an illegal threat, or violates export control laws.
    10-Mail fraud, pyramid schemes.
    11-Redirection to another services.
    12-Asking for personal information about other members from staff member(s) (IP, E-mail, etc.)
    13-Personal information about you or someone else (Addresses, Phone numbers etc...): Displaying or Asking.

We expect a community atmosphere here, and as a result, there are a few things we would like to keep in check:

Advertising

    1- Do not Advertise websites. This includes making a new topic, designed simply to promote a service or website, or posting in an existing one.
    2-You MAY post links to Websites in your Signature. SunShip Forums and it's staff is not liable for any damage caused by pressing the links, you do so at your OWN RISK. We will however, do our best to delete any offending links.
    3-Posting links to
    - interesting sites
    - that you don't own
    - is likely to spark conversation
    - doesn't promote a product
    ...is acceptable. For example: an article found on a website.

Accounts

    1-No Alternative Accounts are allowed on this Forum. Under any circumstance.
    2-The punishment for breaking this rule is typically having the Alternative Account Permanently Banned, and the Main account Temporarily Banned.
    3-Using an Alternative Account to bypass a Ban will result in the ban being incremented or changed to a Permanent Ban.

Posting

    1-DOUBLE POSTING is frowned upon, but may be accepted depending on circumstance:
    - You've run out of allowed images per post.
    - You're creating a Forum Game, or other very large post, and need more space.
    - New information will only make sense if created in an entirely new post. Be careful, Moderators will use their judgement to decide if this is true.

    2- If you have content you feel might be inappropriate but DOESN'T conflict with our rules, don't hesitate to place [NSFW] (Not Safe For Work) into the Topic or next to the Link.


Bumping and Existing Topics

    1-You should always search to make sure the topic you're creating doesn't already exist!
    2- There is nothing in the rules that says Bumping dead topics is not allowed... (you can't even report it), if a topic is dead and someone has revived, don't reply; and it'll stay that way!
    3-HOWEVER, mass bumping, and bumping your own topic is not allowed.
    Bumping your own topic might be allowed when adding content to Galleries (IE. Picture Galleries)
    4-Moderators have the right to lock topics they feel do not need to be bumped any more.

Punishment

    1-Breaking the non-"Insta-ban" rules will usually result in a warning.
    2-A Non-Formal Warning, is when a Moderator or Admin kindly asks you to stop breaking a rule, usually in public.
    3-A Formal Warning is when you're given a Black Mark on your account for the offense. You can't see these, however; Moderators will usually send you a Personal Message when you've got one.
    4-If you get three Formal Warnings you'll be banned temporarily or permanently depending on the severity of your total offenses.



And Remember, you can only make a topic if you have over 50 posts.
Also, no rape jokes are allowed.
