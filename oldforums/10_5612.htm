Welcome to the wonderful world of Online Bulletin Boards or "Forums" for short. Everyone should have a skim of these helpful topics and suggestions:

I signed up, now what?

Well, the first obvious thing is to introduce yourself HERE.

But you might have noticed some people are spelling better than you! But don't worry, there is plenty of alternatives:

- Dictionary for Firefox.
http://www.mozilla-europe.org/en/firefox/
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/browse/type:3

- ieSpell for Internet Explorer
http://www.iespell.com/

You should really consider using these applications, better spelling means people will take you more seriously.

There is also some Netiquette you might need to learn:

Text Speak
Reserve your text speak for texts, and Instant Messengers. People don't like trying to figure out what this means:
4LL 7|-|1$ rUBB1$|-|

Attitude
Stay calm, it's the only way to show you are superior. Swearing and screaming at people will only get you reported.

Posting
When posting a reply, make sure it's significant. Boring posts like: "I agree" won't get you any brownie points.
You should only post if you have something interesting to bring to the topic.

Topics
When making topics, think about how useful or interesting it is. And more importantly, don't ignore the spelling.
Everyone will be trying to understand your point, if you did not make it very well then people will simply make fun of you.

Don't forget that you CAN edit your posts, and as many times as you wish.

Searching
Before posting a topic, you should first make sure that one does not already exist. Failing to do so, will only result in the closure of your own.
